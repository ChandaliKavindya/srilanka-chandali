Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as Lion Rock, is an ancient rock fortress and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in Sri Lanka, offering stunning views and incredible history.

Galle Fort

Built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and fortified by the Dutch, Galle Fort is a living heritage site filled with charming streets, colonial architecture, and vibrant culture.

Ella

Ella is a small town in the Badulla District known for its breathtaking views of lush green hills, tea plantations, and the famous Nine Arches Bridge.

Temple of the Tooth

Located in Kandy, the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is one of the most sacred Buddhist temples in the world, housing the relic of the tooth of the Buddha.
